# Getting Access to Family Radio

### Step 1 — Request access
1. Go to the radio site — you'll be shown a sign-in page with a field for your email address
2. Enter your email address and tap **Continue**
3. You'll see a message that your request is pending — the station owner needs to approve you first

---

### Step 2 — Wait for approval
The station owner will get an email alert and approve your account. You'll receive an email with a sign-in link once approved.

---

### Step 2a — Install the app *(mobile only, if you haven't already)*

**iPhone/iPad:**
1. Open the radio site in **Safari or Chrome**
2. Tap the Share button (Safari) or the three-dot menu (Chrome) → **Add to Home Screen**
3. Tap **Add** — the app will appear on your home screen

**Android:**
1. Open the radio site in **Chrome**
2. Tap the three-dot menu → **Add to Home Screen** (or accept the install banner if it appears)
3. Tap **Add** — the app will appear on your home screen

---

### Step 3 — First sign-in

**On desktop:**
1. Click the link in your approval email — it opens a page with a button
2. Click **Get my sign-in code** — you'll see a 6-digit code
3. Go back to the radio site tab. If you still see the confirmation message, tap the **Back** button on the page to return to the sign-in screen
4. Enter your email and tap **Continue** — you'll now see a field to enter your code
5. Enter the 6-digit code
6. You're in — set your display name when prompted. If you've previously submitted songs to the station, use the same name you submitted under so your song history is linked to your account.

**On mobile:**
1. Tap the link in your approval email — it opens in your mail app's built-in browser and shows a button
2. Tap **Get my sign-in code** — note the 6-digit code shown
3. Open the radio app from your home screen. If you still see the confirmation message, tap the **Back** button on the page to return to the sign-in screen
4. Enter your email and tap **Continue** — you'll now see a field to enter your code
5. Enter the 6-digit code
6. You're in — set your display name when prompted. If you've previously submitted songs to the station, use the same name you submitted under so your song history is linked to your account.

> The 6-digit code expires in 5 minutes, so complete steps 2–5 quickly.

---

### Step 4 — Set up a passkey *(recommended)*

A passkey lets you sign in with Face ID, Touch ID, or your device PIN instead of waiting for an email link every time.

After your first sign-in you'll be prompted to set up a passkey. You can do it right then, or skip it and come back later via **Settings**.

**On iPhone/iPad:**
1. Tap **Add passkey** (at the prompt or in Settings)
2. Follow the Face ID / Touch ID prompt
3. Done — your passkey is saved to iCloud Keychain and will be available on all your Apple devices

**On Android:**
1. Tap **Add passkey** (at the prompt or in Settings)
2. Follow the fingerprint / PIN prompt
3. Done — your passkey is saved to Google Password Manager

**On desktop (Chrome/Edge/Safari):**
1. Click **Add passkey** (at the prompt or in Settings)
2. Follow your browser or OS prompt (Windows Hello, Touch ID on Mac, etc.)
3. Done

---

### Future sign-ins (with passkey)

1. Open the app (mobile) or go to the radio site (desktop) and tap **Sign in**
2. Tap **Sign in with passkey** — your device will prompt for Face ID / Touch ID / PIN
3. You're in immediately — no email needed

---

### Reinstalling or switching devices

If you get a new phone or reinstall the app, your passkey will still be available if you use iCloud Keychain (iPhone) or Google Password Manager (Android) — they sync automatically. If your passkey is gone for any reason, just use the email sign-in flow again to get back in, then add a new passkey.
